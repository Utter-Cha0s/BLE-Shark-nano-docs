---
title: Screensaver
---

The BLEShark Nano features a simple bouncing DVD-style screensaver.  
It helps prevent OLED burn-in while also looking oddly satisfying.

### How to Enable the Screensaver

<ul className="step-list">
  <li>Open the <strong>Extra Settings Mode.</strong></li>
  <li>Connect to the BLEShark Nano's Wi-Fi network.</li>
  <li>Enable the <strong>Screensaver</strong> option.</li>
  <li>Set the <strong>Screensaver Delay</strong> to define when it activates.</li>
</ul>

> A future update will let you upload a custom screensaver icon.
